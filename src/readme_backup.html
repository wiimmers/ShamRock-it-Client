ShamRock.it as a file transfer and compression system

This program makes transferring your files to a new PC quick and easy for everyone involved. (Or as a general backup of your files) ShamRock.it will take your current user files from within 6 different folders on your system. Your current user is:

Desktop, Documents, Downloads, Favorites, Pictures, Videos.

Upon completion of file transfer and compression, you will see a new folder on your C drive titled 'tmp'

If your PC is old or you've had it for a while, consider deleting any files in these folders that you don't need
